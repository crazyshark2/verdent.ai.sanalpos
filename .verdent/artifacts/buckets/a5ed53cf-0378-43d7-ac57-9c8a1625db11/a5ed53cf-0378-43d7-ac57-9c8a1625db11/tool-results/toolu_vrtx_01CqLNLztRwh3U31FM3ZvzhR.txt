## Related Directories
- **Entity**: Data models for POS operations
- **DataMapper**: Maps responses from gateways to entities
- **Serializer**: Handles serialization of gateway requests/responses
- **Factory**: Creates gateway instances
- **Client**: HTTP client for gateway communication

This architecture follows a **Gateway Pattern** with abstraction layers for extensibility across multiple payment processors.
